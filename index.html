My Expertise

I am well-versed in the web design and develop process.

UX/UI

I sketch and wireframe interfaces that focus on simple interactions and common UI patterns, also have a understanding of the values of color.

Responsive

I code websites that are responsive across common platforms such as desktop, tablet and multiple phone screens. I achieve this either by using responsive frameworks such as the Bootstrap-grid or using custom CSS3 media queries.

Code

I design and develop websites with languages such as HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. I am well-versed in Front-end frameworks such as Bootstrap. I am also no stranger to developing websites with a back-end involving NodeJS, ExpressJS and MongoDB.
